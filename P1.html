Genshin Impact

About the game

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play action role-playing game developed by miHoYo, also known as Hoyoverse. It features an anime-style open world environment and an action-based battle system using elemental magic and character-switching. Players can explore seven nations in the fantasy world of Teyvat, each tied to a different element and ruled by a different god. The story follows the Traveler, an interstellar adventurer who is separated from their twin sibling after landing in Teyvat and embarks on a journey to find them.

The game allows players to control one of four characters in a party, switching between them quickly in combat to use different combinations of skills and attacks. Each character has two unique combat skills:

  • Elemental Skill
  • Elemental Burst
The game is monetized through gacha game mechanics, where players can spend in-game currency to obtain characters and items.

The game is available on the following platforms for free;

  1. Android
  2. IOS
  3. Windows
  4. PS4
  5. PS5
  6. XBOX series X/S
