Game Modes

Genshin offers a variety of gameplay modes for players to enjoy, whether casually or intensely.

These modes include;

  1. Open world exploration
  2. Co-op with up to 3 additional real players
  3. Endgame combat challenges in the form of:
    • Spiral abyss Learn more
    • Imaginarium Theatre Learn more
  4. Personal home design through the Serenitea Pot Learn more

...among others

Gameplay Mode Description Permanent?
Co-Op Mode Allows up to four players to adventure together in Teyvat, participate in events, and explore. Yes
Genius Invokation TCG A card game introduced in Version 3.3 where players duel NPCs and collect cards. Yes
Heated Battle Mode Limited-time mode for Genius Invokation TCG with special rules. Yes
Rhythm Game Mode Music rhythm minigames from past events, suggested to be made permanent. Yes
Serenitea Pot A housing system where players customize a personal teapot space. Yes
Custom Gameplay Stages A future creative mode where players can design and share custom stages. No
Spiral Abyss Limited time combat domain where enemies are greatly buffed. Yes
Imaginarium Theater A monthly combat event where predetermined elements and lineups are picked and random teams are made in order to defeat villains. Elementl reactions are usually buffed. Yes
